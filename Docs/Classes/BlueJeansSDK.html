BlueJeansSDK

public class BlueJeansSDK

The BlueJeans iOS Video Software Development Kit (SDK) enables embedding BlueJeans Video and Audio capabilities into iOS apps. BlueJeansSDK is a single namespace that encapsulates all the API’s related to establishing a BlueJeans Video and Audio connection.
