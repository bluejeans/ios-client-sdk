MeetingState

Provides for various meeting states.

  • idle

    Not in a meeting, or disconnected/dropped from the meeting

  • validating

    Intitial request to join API in progress

  • waitingRoom

    The client is in the waiting room, waiting for the moderator to either accept or deny our entry to the meeting.

  • connecting

    Join API successful and now connecting video and audio streams, most in meeting logic available now but values may change

  • reconnecting

    Call connection has dropped and BlueJeans is attempting to reconnect to the meeting.

  • connected

    Fully connected to the call media although updates to roster and chat might be delayed
